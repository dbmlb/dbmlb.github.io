Topic

Technology has been consistently used to change the way goods are delivered by courier/delivery services. This investigation investigates what developments have been made in the South African courier industry and how these changes affect the broader economic climate in a growing country.

Focus

Focus Question

What technological innovations have been implemented to augment and improve delivery/courier services and how have they impacted South Africa’s economy?

Focus Area

The focus of this investigation will discuss how newer technologies play a role in improving things like delivery speed, tracking accuracy, and overall service reliability in South Africa.

This investigation determines the innovations made in technology in courier/delivery services and whether those technological advancements have had a positive or negative effect on the delivery/courier service providers and the wider South African economy.

Abstract of Findings

This investigation discovered and expanded on the way technology is currently being used in Delivery and Courier Services and explored the current advantages and future advancements that are expected in the field.

Investigation Procedure

This investigation was conducted within the expectations set out in the PAT Working Document, the primary research for the task has been collected and stored in a Microsoft Word Planning document which was created in Phase 1. This document contains the relevant information and data required for the investigation. In Phase 2, information was collected through a questionnaire from individuals who make use of delivery services as well as those involved in the industry. The results are summarized and collated in a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet as well as a Microsoft Access database. In the third Phase, this website will presents the information collected in the previous two phases.
