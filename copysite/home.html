Welcome to Technology in Courier Services

Topic

Technology has been consistently used to change the way goods are delivered, from order placement and tracking to sorting and transportation. This investigation will determine what developments have been made in the South African courier industry and how these changes affect the broader economic climate in a growing country. The focus is to examine how newer technology plays a role in improving things like delivery speed, tracking accuracy, and overall service reliability in South Africa.

Focus Question

What technological innovations have been implemented to augment and improve delivery/courier services and how have they impacted South Africa's economy?
